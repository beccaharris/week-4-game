Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game - this will be your "target number" - the number you're trying to reach.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific (and random) number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the target number you are given...You lose the game if your total score goes above the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time that the game starts, the target number changes,as will the values of each individual crystal.

Congratulations! Your score is exactly the same as the target number! You won!
Click a crystal to play again or the reset button to start over.
Oh no! Your total score exceeded the target number! You lose this round!
Click a crystal to play again or the reset button to start over.
